# Research on Herr Kuchen and Kuchen TV

## Herr Kuchen

Herr Kuchen is a German rapper from near Frankfurt.

### Biography
- Started as Lebkuchenpferdchen (Gingerbread Pony) in 2013, participated in BRT 2013.
- First public appearance in 2015 at Videobattleturnier (VBT).
- In 2017, competed in JuliensblogBattle (JBB), reached final, defeated artists like Greeen, collaborated with Jay.
- Known for rap battles, features with artists like 257ers, Jindo.

### Discography
- Jeffrey EP (2015)
- VBT Sampler 2015
- Other albums and EPs, average rating around 2.00 on RateYourMusic.

### Social Media
- Instagram: @herrzudemkuchen
- Facebook: @herrkuchen
- YouTube: herrkuchen
- Spotify: Artist page

### Controversies
- Involved in disputes with other rappers, e.g., Jindo had to pay a four-figure sum.
- Seen as controversial in German rap scene.

## Kuchen TV

Kuchen TV is a German YouTube and Twitch creator.

### Biography
- Makes videos criticizing populism, right-wing influencers.
- Videos include titles like "Deutschland ist so am Ende" (Germany is so done), "Messerstecher und Vergewaltiger in Deutschland" (Knife attackers and rapists in Germany).
- Claims to criticize right-wing parties like AfD.

### Platforms
- YouTube: kuchentv
- Twitch: kuchentv
- Instagram: @kuchentv
- Spotify: Artist page (possibly for music related)

## Similarities
- Both have "Kuchen" (cake) in their names, which is German for cake.
- Both are German entertainers in digital media.
- Both started gaining attention around similar times (mid-2010s).
- No direct connection, but names are similar, leading to occasional confusion (e.g., people mistaking one for the other).
- Both have rap/music elements: Herr Kuchen is a rapper, Kuchen TV has a Spotify page, possibly uploads music.
- Videos and publications: Both produce content, Herr Kuchen in rap battles/videos, Kuchen TV in YouTube videos on social issues.
- No known collaborations or direct similarities in content, but both address German culture/society.

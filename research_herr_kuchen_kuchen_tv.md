# Research on Herr Kuchen and Kuchen TV

## Herr Kuchen

Herr Kuchen is a German rapper from near Frankfurt.

### Biography
- Real name: Marc-Philipp Müller, born 1996 near Frankfurt.
- Started as Lebkuchenpferdchen (Gingerbread Pony) in 2013, participated in BRT 2013.
- First public appearance in 2015 at Videobattleturnier (VBT).
- In 2017, competed in JuliensblogBattle (JBB), reached final, defeated artists like Greeen, collaborated with Jay.
- Known for rap battles, features with artists like 257ers, Jindo.
- Debut album "Frisch aus dem Ofen" topped Hiphop.de Newcomer charts. Second album "Frisch aus dem Ofen zwei" released end of 2019.

### Discography
- 14 albums total, 247 songs.
- Notable albums: Jeffrey EP (2015), Der Tote EP (underground tape), Frisch aus dem Ofen (debut, #1 on Hiphop.de Newcomer charts), Frisch aus dem Ofen zwei (2019).
- Popular songs: Zisar, Jay Jiggy, Herr Kuchen, EnteTainment.
- Collaborations: With Jay, 257ers, Jindo.
- Average rating around 2.00 on RateYourMusic.

### Social Media
- Instagram: @herrzudemkuchen
- Facebook: @herrkuchen
- YouTube: herrkuchen
- Spotify: Artist page

### Notable Videos and Battles
- VBT 2015 qualification and participation.
- JBB 2018 final vs Tamas (lost 4:11).
- Battles: vs Liuz, vs Jindo (Jindo paid four-figure sum due to dispute), other underground battles.
- Videos on YouTube channel: Battle highlights, interviews, e.g., Herr Kuchen vs Liuz interview.

### Lyrics and Themes
- Themes: Irony, technical punchlines, oldschool head-nodding beats, drug use and addiction (e.g., song 'Methylendioxymethylamphetamin'), daily life, battles.
- Notable songs: Nudeln, Daily Bizznes, Plastikshit II, Jana (diss track), Tante Emma.
- Style: Satirical, provocative, humorous lines.

### Lyrics Analysis
- Song 'Methylendioxymethylamphetamin' (from Spritziges Gebäck, 2021): Explores drug use and addiction, acknowledges controversy but sees it as integral to creative process. Lyrics: 'Und auch wenn du 'ne Meinung hast zu Drogen, dann wird die in Mitleidenschaft gezogen / Denn guck, hier wird mit Leidenschaft gezogen' (Even if you have an opinion on drugs, it will be compromised / Because here, it's drawn with passion).
- Overall themes: Drug addiction, daily life struggles, battles, with ironic and punchy delivery.

### Collaborations
- Groups: Kollabo (with Zisar, Jay Jiggy, MiZeb, kankama, 4Tune, Deetox Vengeance, JACK VON CRACK, Gio, Raportagen), Paragraph 84 Allstars, Plastikshit II, Roter Faden, Lidotaxi.
- Features: With Jay, 257ers, Jindo, Genetikk (e.g., 'Tanke in Frankreich').
- Ex-partner Jindo109: Former duo, now in dispute.

### Controversies
- Involved in disputes with other rappers, e.g., Jindo paid four-figure sum; diss tracks like 'Jana' accusing ex-partner of manipulation, domestic violence, drug addiction.

### Fan Discussions
- Reddit threads in r/GermanRap: 'Herr Kuchen vs Alle?' discussing his reputation; disputes with Jindo and Jana; collaborations like with Genetikk.
- Seen as controversial, with fans debating his behavior and tracks.

### Current Status
- Active as of 2026, releasing albums in 2026, 2024, 2023.
- Ongoing dispute with ex-partner Jindo109, involving diss tracks and public exchanges.
- Continues producing music and videos.

### Net Worth
- Not publicly disclosed, but as an underground rapper, estimated lower than major YouTubers; likely in the tens to hundreds of thousands based on career.

## Kuchen TV

Kuchen TV is a German YouTube and Twitch creator.

### Biography
- Real name: Tim Heldt, born ~1995, from Braunschweig.
- Started as Kuchengeschmack (Cake Flavor), participated in VBT 3 times.
- Rebranded to KuchenTV around 2011-2012.
- Now a YouTuber and Twitch streamer, known for opinion videos, often satirical or critical of media, politics, society.
- Makes videos criticizing populism, right-wing influencers.
- Videos include titles like "Deutschland ist so am Ende" (Germany is so done), "Messerstecher und Vergewaltiger in Deutschland" (Knife attackers and rapists in Germany).
- Claims to criticize right-wing parties like AfD.

### Music
- As Kuchengeschmack, participated in rap battles with satirical, ironic, provocative lines.
- No major discography, but battle tracks and possibly YouTube uploads.

### Notable Videos
- "Die Zerstörung von Imp und Dekarldent" (Destruction of Imp and Dekarldent)
- "Der Postillon24, schlimmer als die Bild" (Postillon24, worse than Bild)
- "Soll Bargeld abgeschafft werden?" (Should cash be abolished?)
- "Alphakevin - Kuchen Talks" series (talk shows with guests)
- Videos on Twitch: Live streams, possibly archived.

### Controversies
- Criticized on Reddit for using ADHS as excuse for problematic behavior, e.g., not finishing work on time.
- Accused of populism in video titles and content, though claims to criticize right-wing influencers and AfD.
- Admitted to fabricating stories in videos, leading to discussions on honesty and self-reflection.
- Seen as controversial in German YouTube scene for 'uninformierte und teils rechtspopulistische Takes'.

### Influences
- Started in battle rap as Kuchengeschmack, influenced by VBT and rap battles.
- Transitioned to YouTube opinion videos, inspired by media criticism and social commentary.

### Collaborations/Guests
- Music collaborations: With Timothy Dece on 'Viva La Despacito'.
- YouTube guests: In 'Kuchen Talks' series, e.g., Alphakevin.
- No major rap collaborations post-transition.

### Collaborations/Mentions
- No direct collaborations or mentions with Herr Kuchen found. Separate paths in rap and YouTube.

### Fan Discussions
- Reddit threads in r/Laesterschwestern, r/Papaplatte: Discussions on using ADHS as excuse, bans on Twitch, controversies with Shurjoka, problematic behavior.
- Fans defend or criticize his content, with debates on populism and honesty.

### Current Status
- Active as of 2026, producing YouTube videos and Twitch streams.
- Faced legal issues in 2024: Strafbefehl for using unconstitutional symbols, claimed accidental slip in livestream (said 'Sieg Heil' instead of 'Qualität').
- Continues commentary videos, talk shows like 'Kuchen Talks'.

### Net Worth
- Estimated $1.5M - $2M as of 2026.
- Monthly earnings: $18K - $782K from YouTube ads.
- Annual earnings: $422K - $9.5M.
- Sources: Ad revenue, sponsorships, Twitch.

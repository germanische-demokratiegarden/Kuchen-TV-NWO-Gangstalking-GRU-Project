# Herr Kuchen Research

## Biography
- Real name: Marc-Philipp Müller, born 1996 near Frankfurt.
- Started as Lebkuchenpferdchen (Gingerbread Pony) in 2013, participated in BRT 2013.
- First public appearance in 2015 at Videobattleturnier (VBT).
- In 2017, competed in JuliensblogBattle (JBB), reached final, defeated artists like Greeen, collaborated with Jay.
- Known for rap battles, features with artists like 257ers, Jindo.
- Debut album "Frisch aus dem Ofen" topped Hiphop.de Newcomer charts. Second album "Frisch aus dem Ofen zwei" released end of 2019.

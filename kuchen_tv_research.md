# Kuchen TV Research

## Biography
- Real name: Tim Heldt, born April 19, 1995, from Braunschweig.
- Started as Kuchengeschmack (Cake Flavor), participated in VBT 3 times.
- Rebranded to KuchenTV around 2011-2012.
- Now a YouTuber and Twitch streamer, known for opinion videos, often satirical or critical of media, politics, society.
- Makes videos criticizing populism, right-wing influencers.
- Videos include titles like "Deutschland ist so am Ende" (Germany is so done), "Messerstecher und Vergewaltiger in Deutschland" (Knife attackers and rapists in Germany).
- Claims to criticize right-wing parties like AfD.
